Tasty avocado-tomato-chicken bagel

Hard to make your boyfriend eat vegetable? You don’t like avocado? You children only want to eat bread?
Here is a recipe really easy, really tasty, and that everyone would eat without problem.

What do you need? Simple

  • an avocado
  • A tomato
  • A bagel (I personally prefer the sesame one but any would fit)
  • Chicken slices
  • Cheese slice (your favourite one)

How to do it? Even more simple

Step 1 : cut the avocado and tomato in slices.
Step 2 : Put the bagel on a baking paper, and put all the ingredients between the bagel’s slices.
Step 3 : Bake for 10min, just time for the cheese to melt and for the bagel to toast.

This recipe is perfect for broke students or cooks in a rush, you’ll get tasty sandwich a full stomach for just few euros/pounds/dollars.
Enjoy your meal !!!
